Timeline of A&T

  • 1891 - North Carolina A&T was founded.

  • 1925 - Ferdinand D. Bluford selected as third president.

  • 1957 - College becomes "Agricultural and Technical College of North Carolina".

  • 1960 - Samuel D. Proctor selected as fifth president.

  • 1968 - Aggies football team wins its second HBCU National Championship.

  • 1999 - Dr. James C. Renick is installed as the ninth chancellor.

  • 2009 - Dr. Harold L. Martin, Sr. becomes chancellor.

  • 2012 - Michelle Obama, First Lady of the United States, comes to speak at A&T.

  • 2016 - Barack Obama, Former President, comes to A&T.

  • 2022 - Aggies join the CAA Athletic Conference.
